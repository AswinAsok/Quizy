Instructions

  • There will be a Total of 10 Question related to the Option you Choose default choose is JavaScript.
  • Your Total Points will be displayed at the end of the Quiz only.
  • Once attempted question will not be available for a Reattempt.
  • All the Questions will be having Multiple Choice.
  • Points will not be deducted for Wrong answer.
  • Once the page is refreshed all your Progress will be lost.
  • There is No Time Limit so take your own time.
  • Un-Attempted Question will be awarded 0 points.
  • Once have selected the category you can start the quiz by clicking the Start Button.
